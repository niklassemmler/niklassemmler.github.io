About Me

I am Niklas, a PhD student at SAP SE in Potsdam and expect to graduate in 2020.

I focus on data management issues for the future Industrial Internet of Things. If you want to know more, head over to the research section.

During my PhD I helped to organize an introduction to programming course with ~800 students and became a member in the Software Campus, a German program that supports PhD with a 2-year leadership programme. As member of the Berlin Big Data Center (BBDC) project I studied the performance of Apache Flink for straggling tasks. You can get more info on me.

Personally, I am interested in a wide range of topics and try to write about it on my blog. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to send me a mail.
